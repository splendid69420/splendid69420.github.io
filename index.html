Our Recommendations

RED ROASTER is a coffee blend that is known for its bold and rich flavor. According to the manufacturer, the blend is made from high-quality Arabica beans sourced from different regions around the world, including South America, Central America, and Africa. The beans are roasted in small batches to ensure optimal flavor and freshness.

Many coffee enthusiasts appreciate the smooth taste and aroma of BACK PORCH DECAF. The coffee is said to have a medium body and balanced acidity, with notes of chocolate and caramel. It's also worth noting that the decaffeination process used to make this coffee is a natural Swiss Water process, which doesn't involve any chemicals, making it a healthier choice for those who are sensitive to caffeine.

HIGHLANDS BLEND is a premium coffee blend that features a unique combination of Arabica beans sourced from the highlands of various regions around the world, including Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. The blend is known for its rich and complex flavor profile, which includes notes of dark chocolate, caramel, and a hint of citrus.

VIENNA COFFEE HOUSE DECAF is a decaffeinated coffee blend that is inspired by the rich and flavorful coffee traditions of Vienna, Austria. The coffee is made from high-quality Arabica beans that are carefully selected and roasted to perfection to create a smooth and satisfying cup of coffee, without the caffeine.

Front Porch Breakfast Blend is a smooth medium roast with a mellow flavor and hints of chocolate and nuts. Perfect for your morning cup, it pairs well with your favorite breakfast. Enjoy it black or with cream and sugar, and savor the aroma of freshly baked pastries. Wake up and enjoy a cup on your front porch or wherever you love to savor your coffee.

The Vienna Coffee House Blend is often described as having notes of dark chocolate, caramel, and toasted nuts, with a subtle hint of sweetness. It is a versatile blend that can be enjoyed black or with milk and sugar, making it a great choice for coffee lovers of all tastes.
